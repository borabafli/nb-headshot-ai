1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Headshot Generator ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and services (collectively, the "Service").

By using AI Headshot Generator, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use the Service.

Generated Headshots: We store the AI-generated headshot images you create using our Service. These images are stored securely in your personal account.

Image Metadata: We may collect metadata associated with your images, such as creation date, selected style options (clothing, expression, background), and generation settings.

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the Service:

  • Generation count and usage statistics
  • Subscription tier and status (free, weekly, or monthly plan)
  • Device information (device type, operating system, app version)
  • App performance data and crash reports
  • Feature usage patterns and preferences

2.3 Payment Information

If you purchase a subscription, payment processing is handled by third-party payment processors (Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and RevenueCat). We do not directly collect or store your credit card or payment information. Please refer to the privacy policies of these payment providers for details on how they handle your payment data.

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not require or collect:

  • Personal account creation (no email or username required)
  • Contact information (phone number, email address) unless you contact support

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Location

Local Device Storage: Your generation history, usage statistics, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using secure encrypted storage.

Cloud Storage (Supabase): Your generated headshot images are stored securely in our cloud storage infrastructure (Supabase) to enable access across devices and ensure data persistence.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Encryption at Rest: Your images are encrypted when stored in our cloud infrastructure
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access your data
  • Regular Security Audits: We perform regular security reviews and updates
  • Isolated Storage: Each user's images are isolated and inaccessible to other users

4.3 Data Processing Security

During AI generation, your images are:

  • Processed on secure servers with industry-standard encryption
  • Never stored permanently during the processing phase (only temporarily cached)
  • Deleted from processing servers once generation is complete
  • Only accessible by authorized AI processing systems

5. Image Processing and AI

5.1 AI Technology

AI Headshot Generator uses advanced artificial intelligence technology to transform your photos into professional headshots. The AI analyzes your facial features, pose, and lighting to generate realistic professional portraits.

5.2 Processing Workflow

  1. Upload: You upload a photo from your device
  2. Analysis: Our AI analyzes the image to identify facial features and characteristics
  3. Generation: The AI generates a new professional headshot based on your selected style options
  4. Storage: The generated headshot is saved to your device and our secure cloud storage
  5. Cleanup: Temporary processing files are deleted from our servers

5.3 AI Model Training

Important: We do NOT use your uploaded photos or generated headshots to train our AI models without your explicit consent. Your images are only used to provide the Service to you.

We may use aggregated, anonymized usage data (not including your actual images) to improve our Service and AI model performance.

8. Data Deletion

8.1 In-App Data Deletion

You can delete all your data directly within the app:

  1. Open the AI Headshot Generator app
  2. Go to Settings (⚙️ icon)
  3. Scroll down to "Data & Privacy" section
  4. Tap "Delete All Data"
  5. Confirm the deletion

This will permanently delete:

  • All your generated headshots from our cloud storage
  • Your generation history and usage statistics
  • All locally stored data on your device

8.2 Manual Deletion Request

If you're unable to delete your data through the app, you can contact us at nikolai.bora.mindwise@gmail.com with a deletion request. We will process your request within 30 days.

8.3 What Happens After Deletion

  • Immediate: Your data is marked for deletion and becomes inaccessible
  • Within 7 days: All your images are permanently deleted from our cloud storage
  • Within 30 days: All associated data and backups are completely removed
  • Exceptions: Some data may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., financial records for tax compliance)

For detailed step-by-step instructions, see our Data Deletion Guide.
