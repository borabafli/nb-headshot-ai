Deleting your data from AI Headshot Generator is permanent and cannot be undone. This action will:

  • Permanently delete all your generated headshots from our cloud storage
  • Remove your generation history from your device
  • Erase all usage statistics and preferences
  • Delete your original photos from our servers (if any were temporarily stored)

There is no way to recover your data after deletion.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your data, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Generated Headshots: All AI-generated professional headshots stored in our cloud
  • Generation History: Your complete generation history and metadata
  • Original Photos: Any temporarily stored original photos
  • Style Preferences: Your selected clothing, expression, and background preferences
  • Usage Statistics: Generation counts, subscription usage, and activity data
  • Local Data: All app data stored on your device

What's NOT deleted: Subscription records (required for billing and legal compliance) and payment information (handled by Apple/Google, not stored by us).

How to Delete Your Data

1

Open AI Headshot Generator App

Launch the AI Headshot Generator mobile application on your iOS or Android device.

2

You'll see a second confirmation dialog asking "Are You Sure?"

The message will remind you: "This will permanently delete all your generated headshots, history, and data. This cannot be undone."

8

Deletion Process

The app will:

  1. Show a "Deleting Data" progress indicator
  2. Remove all images from cloud storage (Supabase)
  3. Clear all local device data
  4. Reset usage counters and preferences

This process may take a few seconds to a minute depending on how much data you have.

9

Data Deleted Successfully

You'll see a success message confirming that all your data has been deleted.

The app will return to a fresh state, as if you just installed it for the first time.

You can continue using the app and generate new headshots whenever you're ready.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Immediate: All your data is marked for deletion and becomes inaccessible
  • Within 24 hours: Your images are permanently deleted from our cloud storage (Supabase)
  • Within 7 days: All backups and cached data are completely removed
  • Subscription: Your subscription status remains unchanged - you can still generate new headshots
  • Fresh Start: You can immediately start using the app again with a clean slate
  • No Recovery: Deleted data cannot be restored or recovered

Alternative: Delete Individual Images

Don't want to delete everything?

If you only want to delete specific generated headshots without removing all your data:

  1. Go to the History section in the app
  2. Find the image you want to delete
  3. Tap on the image to open it
  4. Tap the Delete button (trash icon)
  5. Confirm the deletion

This will delete only that specific image while keeping the rest of your data intact.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I recover my data after deletion?

No. Once deleted, your data is permanently removed from our systems and cannot be recovered. We don't maintain backups of user-deleted data.

Will deleting data cancel my subscription?

No. Deleting your data does not affect your subscription status. You'll remain subscribed and can continue generating new headshots. If you want to cancel your subscription, you must do so separately through your Apple App Store or Google Play Store account settings.

What happens to images I've downloaded?

Images you've already downloaded and saved to your device's photo library or other locations will not be affected. Data deletion only removes images from our cloud storage and the app - it doesn't delete files you've saved elsewhere.

How long does deletion take?

The in-app deletion process takes a few seconds to a minute. Complete removal from all our systems (including backups) is completed within 7 days.

Will my subscription history be deleted?

No. Subscription records, payment transactions, and billing history are retained as required by law for accounting and tax purposes (typically 7 years). However, these records are separate from your generated images and usage data.

Can I use the app after deleting my data?

Yes! You can continue using the app immediately after deleting your data. You'll start with a fresh slate and can generate new headshots according to your subscription plan.
